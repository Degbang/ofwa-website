Collaborate With Us

Partner With OFWA

Together we can close the knowledge gap faster. We're looking for organisations that share our commitment to open access, equity, and Africa's digital future.

The Case for Partnership

Why Partner With OFWA?

01
Trusted Community Reach

We have established relationships with communities, universities, schools, and media across Ghana and West Africa.

02
Proven Impact

We can demonstrate measurable outcomes — articles written, editors trained, schools reached, and communities served.

03
Aligned with Global Goals

Our work aligns with the UN SDGs on quality education, gender equality, and reduced inequalities.

04
Flexible Partnership Models

From project sponsorship to institutional MOUs — we tailor collaboration structures to your organisation's goals.

Partnership Models

How We Can Work Together

🏢

Corporate Sponsorship

Sponsor our events, training programs, or hub operations. Ideal for companies with CSR commitments to education and digital equity.

🎓

Institutional MOU

Universities and schools can partner to establish Wikipedia clubs, host edit-a-thons, and integrate open knowledge into curricula.

🌍

NGO & Civil Society

Joint program delivery, shared resources, and co-advocacy for open access and digital rights across Africa.

🏛️

Government & Institutional

We work with government agencies to digitise heritage, create public knowledge resources, and train civil servants in open tools.
